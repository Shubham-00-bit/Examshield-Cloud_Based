How It Works

Six layers of protection — no single point of failure

01

Unique Paper Per Student

Same questions, different order for every candidate. Copying answers becomes impossible.

02

Immutable On-Chain Records

Every exam creation and registration is stored on Ethereum — tamper-proof, forever verifiable.

03

Time-Locked Access

Papers only unlock during the exact scheduled window. Early access is cryptographically impossible.

04

Device Fingerprinting

Candidates are bound to the device they registered from. No proxy, no switching, no sharing.

05

NEET & JEE Isolated

Fully separate pipelines, databases, and blockchain records for each exam type.

06

Full Audit Trail

Every login, device mismatch, and submission is logged with timestamp and IP address.
